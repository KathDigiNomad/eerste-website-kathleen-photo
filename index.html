My beloved Antwerp Photography

As I live in Antwerp, I have at hand one of the most beautiful sceneries of Belgium. Antwerp is a vibrant city where there is always something to do and to see. A casual stroll here will always turn out to be a walk through the past, the present, new vibes and livelyness.

To travel is to live to the fullest for me, so I dedicate as much time as I can travelling around the globe and exploring new things and corners of the world.

Product Photography

Product photography has become a new interest field for me and I like to explore the texture and look of items. Playing with the lightsettings has become a new way of seeing new possibilities and features.
